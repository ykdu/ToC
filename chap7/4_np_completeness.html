These problems are called NP-complete.

定义7.34（NP-complete）

A language BBB is NP-complete if it satisfies two conditions:

  1. BBB is in NP, and
  2. every AAA in NP is polynomial time reducible to BBB
定理7.35（NP-complete和P=NP?的关系）

If BBB is NP-complete and B∈PB\in PB∈P, then P=NP.

定理7.36（NP-complete内的等价关系）

If BBB is NP-complete and B≤PCB\leq_PCB≤​P​​C for CCC in NP, then CCC is NP-complete.
